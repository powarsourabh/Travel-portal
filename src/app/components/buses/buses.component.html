Popular Destinations

Pune

Pune is a bustling city in the western Indian state of Maharashtra, known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and academic prominence. It's often referred to as the "Oxford of the East" due to its well-respected universities and colleges.

Mumbai

Mumbai, the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra, is a bustling metropolis renowned for its vibrant culture, historic landmarks, and as the heart of the Bollywood film industry.

Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a vibrant metropolis that blends an ancient cultural heritage with modernity. Known for its rich history and architectural marvels, Delhi is home to several UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital city of the Indian state of Telangana, is known for its rich history, culture, and architectural grandeur. Renowned as the "City of Pearls" and "City of Nizams," Hyderabad boasts a unique blend of ancient heritage and modernity.

Bangalore

Bangalore, also known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is renowned as the "Silicon Valley of India" due to its thriving IT industry and numerous tech companies

Kolkata

Kolkata, also known as Calcutta, is a vibrant and culturally rich city located in the eastern part of India, in the state of West Bengal. It is known for its historical significance as the former capital of British India and for being the birthplace of iconic figures like Rabindranath Tagore and Mother Teresa.
