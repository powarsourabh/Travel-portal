About us

Ticket 24 is India’s largest online bus and Flight ticketing platform that has transformed bus travel in the country by bringing ease and convenience to millions of Indians who travel using buses. Founded in 2024, Ticket 24 is part of India’s leading online travel company MakeMyTrip Limited (NASDAQ: MMYT). By providing widest choice, superior customer service, lowest prices and unmatched benefits, Ticket 24 has served over 18 million customers. Ticket 24 has a global presence with operations across Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Colombia and Peru apart from India.
